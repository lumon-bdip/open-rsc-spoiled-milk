# Retired, Replaced, And Repurposed Items

This file tracks item families that are no longer part of active standard play,
plus families whose visuals or item identities are being reused for new roles.

## Retired From Standard Progression

- `Chain` equipment
  - removed from active standard progression
  - chain palettes/silhouettes are being reused for leather armor work
- `Medium helms`
  - removed from active standard progression
  - may remain only for explicit special-case exceptions such as some dragon
    content
- `Square shields`
  - removed from active standard progression
  - should remain dormant for possible later reuse
- `Metal skirts`
  - removed from active standard progression
  - replaced conceptually by body-type-aware visuals on the standard leg items
- `Black` equipment as a standard ladder tier
  - retired from standard progression
  - may be reused later for specialty content
- `White` equipment as a standard ladder tier
  - retired from standard progression
  - may be reused later for specialty content
- standard `Battlestaff` / orb upgrade line
  - retired from standard progression
- old vanilla elemental staff names such as `Staff of air` / `Staff of fire`
  - replaced by rune-first standard-staff products such as `Air Staff` and
    `Fire Staff`
  - standard staff attunement remains an active tier-1 path

## Replaced By New Standard Families

- old `rune as top normal tier`
  - replaced by the `10`-tier standard ladder with `rune` at tier `10`
- old low metal start at `bronze`
  - replaced by the `tin -> copper -> bronze -> ... -> rune` ladder
- old direct jewelry enchant spell outputs
  - replaced by altar-based enchanting
- old elemental-staff progression assumptions
  - replaced by the wood-tier staff ladder plus altar attunement

## Repurposed Or Planned For Repurpose

- chain visuals/palettes
  - being reused for leather armor work
- some clothing/robe visuals
  - may be repurposed later for prayer-aligned or god-aligned equipment
- `Black` / `White` item families
  - may return only as specialty content, not as standard progression

## Special Exceptions

- some `Dragon` tier `11` content may still keep old counterpart identities like
  `Square shield` or `Medium helm`
- quest-only compatibility items may survive temporarily even when their family
  is retired from standard progression

## Maintenance Rule

When a family moves between:

- active
- dormant compatibility
- repurposed visual source
- specialty-only

record that change here so future audits stop treating it as an unresolved
standard progression item.
