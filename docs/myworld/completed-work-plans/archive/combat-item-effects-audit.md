# Combat Item Effects Audit

This audit classifies every currently equipable item against the planned phase-1 combat rework.

Classification rules:
- `List 1`: equipables that do not participate in current combat math and do not appear to need combat-specific follow-up.
- `List 2`: equipables that will be affected because their combat stats or combat requirements will change, but they do not appear to have extra item-specific behavior that needs bespoke handling.
- `List 3`: equipables that are affected and also have explicit code hooks, spell interactions, or combat perks that need manual review.

Notes:
- This audit is about the phase-1 combat rewrite only.
- `List 1` can still include quest, disguise, cosmetic, or utility items. The point is that the combat rewrite should not force additional item-specific work for them.
- `List 2` mainly covers standard armor, weapons, bows, thrown weapons, and straightforward combat-stat jewelry.
- `List 3` exists because these items are referenced directly in combat, spell, dragonfire, or cape-effect code and cannot safely be handled as pure data remaps.

Special-attention reason buckets:
- Combat skillcape perks in [SkillCapes.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/content/SkillCapes.java) and combat formula/event handlers.
- Dragonfire-specific mitigation in [DragonFireBreath.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/event/rsc/impl/combat/scripts/all/DragonFireBreath.java).
- Spellcasting restrictions or rune-substitution behavior in [SpellHandler.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/net/rsc/handlers/SpellHandler.java) and [Equipment.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/model/container/Equipment.java).
- Reflect damage behavior for Ring of recoil in [CombatEvent.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/event/rsc/impl/combat/CombatEvent.java) and [ProjectileEvent.java](/home/justin/Core-Framework/server/src/com/openrsc/server/event/rsc/impl/projectile/ProjectileEvent.java).

Counts:
- `List 1`: 106 items
- `List 2`: 222 items
- `List 3`: 29 items
