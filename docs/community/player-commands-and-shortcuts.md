# Spoiled Milk Player Commands and Shortcuts

## Chat

- `::g message` - Send a global chat message.
- `::gq` - Show global chat messages in the Quest tab.
- `::gp` - Show global chat messages in the Private tab.
- `::toggleglobalchat` - Toggle global chat on or off.
- `::setglobalmessagecolor color` - Change your global chat message color.
- `::d message` - Send a message to Discord, if Discord chat is enabled.
- `::pair` - Pair your game account with Discord.
- `::showitem item name` or `::showoff item name` - Show off an owned item in Discord.

## Party & Clan

- `::p message` - Send a party chat message.
- `::partyaccept` - Accept a party invite.
- `::leaveparty` - Leave your current party.
- `::shareexp` - Toggle shared experience for the party.
- `::c message` - Send a clan chat message.
- `::claninvite player` - Invite a player to your clan.
- `::clanaccept` - Accept a clan invite.
- `::clankick player` - Kick a player from your clan.
- `::joinclan clan_name` - Request to join a clan.

## Info

- `::commands` - Show available commands.
- `::online` - Show how many players are online.
- `::onlinelist` - Show the online player list.
- `::uniqueonline` - Show how many unique players are online.
- `::coords` - Show your current coordinates.
- `::gameinfo` - Show account and server info.
- `::time`, `::date`, or `::datetime` - Show the server time/date.
- `::kc npc name` or `::kills npc name` - Check your kill count for an NPC.
- `::minigamelog` - View minigame records.
- `::event` - Travel to the active server event, if one is available.
- `::drop` or `::checkholidaydrop` - Check holiday drop status.
- `::gang` - Check your Shield of Arrav gang.

## Convenience

- `::b` or `::bank` - Open the bank while standing in a bank area.
- `::togglereceipts` - Toggle shop/trade receipt messages.
- `::togglenpckcmessages` - Toggle NPC kill count messages.
- `::toggleblockchat` - Toggle public chat blocking.
- `::toggleblockprivate` - Toggle private message blocking.
- `::toggleblocktrade` - Toggle trade request blocking.
- `::toggleblockduel` - Toggle duel request blocking.

## Client Shortcuts

- `Page Up` / `Page Down` - Tilt the camera.
- `Home` - Reset the camera to north/default tilt.
- Mouse wheel, zoom buttons, or arrow keys - Zoom in and out.
- `Ctrl + click` a banker - Open the bank directly.
- `Ctrl + click` a shop NPC - Open the shop directly.
- `Ctrl + click` a ground item - Pick up matching items on that tile until your inventory is full or that item stack is gone.
- Click `Board` on ships - Travel directly if you meet the requirements, skipping the travel dialogue.

## Loot

- NPC drops are personal.
- Everyone who deals damage to an NPC can receive their own drop roll.
- Party loot sharing does not need to be toggled on.
- Summon damage counts toward the summon owner's damage credit.
