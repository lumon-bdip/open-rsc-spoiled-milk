# Idea Submissions

Ideas start in `inbox/` and are reviewed by the owner.

## Status Folders

- `inbox/`: new submissions waiting for review.
- `accepted/`: useful ideas worth keeping.
- `parked/`: ideas that may fit later.
- `rejected/`: ideas the project decided not to pursue.
- `templates/`: reusable submission templates.

Accepted ideas are not automatically approved for development. The owner may
later promote an accepted idea into `docs/myworld/proposed-work-plans/`.
